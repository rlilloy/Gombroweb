About Raul Lilloy

I wrote Kolón. I keep writing — I prefer writing to publishing. Since 2015 I run Incubook.com, a writing school. Gombro is the editor that fits me: no AI, local only, paragraph-centered, dark screen, sentence isolation, literary diary — inspired by Borges's manuscripts of El Aleph.
